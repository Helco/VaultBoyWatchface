VaultBoy watch for pebble|
-------------------------+

Overview:
This is the VaultBoy watch as seen in the Tranquility Lane in Fallout 3.
The watch is configurable as normal,as inverted, with night-indicator and
as inverted with night-indicator. The day span can be configured too
(default 8->20) but to change this you have to recompile the watchface.
